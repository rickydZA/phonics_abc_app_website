English ABCs

🐛 Bug Report

🐛 問題回報

Found a problem? Please tell us! You don't need to be technical — just describe what happened in your own words. Every report helps us fix issues faster.

發現問題了嗎？請告訴我們！您不需要懂技術——只需用自己的話描述發生了什麼。每份回報都能幫助我們更快修復問題。

What Happened?

發生了什麼？

Example: "The sound didn't play when I tapped the letter A."

例如：「點擊字母 A 時沒有播放聲音。」

How Did It Happen?

如何發生的？

Example: "I opened the app, tapped letter B, and the image didn't appear."

例如：「我打開應用程式，點擊字母 B，但圖片沒有出現。」

Your Device

您的裝置

Follow-up (Optional)

後續聯絡（選填）

We may reach out if we need more details to fix the issue.

如果我們需要更多資訊來修復問題，可能會與您聯繫。

Thank You! 🙏

謝謝您！🙏

Your bug report has been submitted. We'll look into it and work on a fix as soon as possible!

您的問題回報已成功提交。我們會盡快調查並修復！
